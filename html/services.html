Our current scope of professional services is focused on the practice areas of Planning, Design, and Graphics. Project types include both new construction and renovation for residential, retail, commercial, industrial, and agricultural building facilities.

Current project work includes multiple residential, retail and commercial projects incorporating wood frame, masonry and steel framed structures, and reinforced concrete foundation designs. Many of these projects incorporate the use of environmentally sustainable design strategies, building materials and systems, such as structural insulated panels (SIPS), insulated concrete forms (ICF), pozolons, engineered wood products, and straw bale and rammed earth construction.

Residential project clients include Architects, builders, contractors, Realtors and homeowners, for projects entailing new home designs, additions, renovations, garages, decks, sunrooms, and site landscape retaining walls.

Commercial project clients include Architects, contractors, developers, owners, agri-business entities, and commercial sign companies.

Additional professional services clients include local city government departments, public schools and churches.

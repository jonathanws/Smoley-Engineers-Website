Raymond A. Smoley

CEO, Professional Engineer

a little history

SMOLEY ENGINEERS & CONSULTANTS, LLC is a multi-faceted planning, engineering and consulting services firm founded in 1999 upon an extensive background of over twenty-nine years experience in architectural and structural engineering and design, construction management, administrative services and graphics design, serving owners, architects, engineers, builders, developers, Realtors, and businesses from many different business sectors.


Design philosophy

SMOLEY ENGINEERS & CONSULTANTS, LLC's engineering and design philosophy has evolved from a comprehensive systems approach to planning and design that acknowledges each project to be an integral and interrelated part of a larger and broader system, whose many internal and external factors influence it's performance, and consequently it's design.

As an extension of that philosophy, we also recognize an increasingly inherent responsibility to design from a holistic point of view, placing a greater importance on sustainability in the design process, and by utilizing renewable materials, products and systems, where possible, to design structures that lessen their impact on the environment, and consequently, their burden on future generations as well.

Professional Engineering licenses are currently maintained for the State of Maryland, and the Commonwealths of Pennsylvania, Virginia, and West Virginia.
